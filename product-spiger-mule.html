The Spiger Mule is our love letter to the Moscow Mule ‚Äî reimagined without a drop of alcohol. We start with freshly grated, raw ginger root to deliver that signature spicy warmth from the very first sip. Hand-squeezed lime juice adds a sharp, citrusy counterpoint that brightens the entire profile. We top it off with craft-brewed, zero-alcohol ginger beer that brings serious carbonation and peppery complexity. A careful dash of non-alcoholic aromatic bitters ties everything together with depth and intrigue. Served in our signature style over crushed ice, the Spiger Mule is designed for those who want intensity, not intoxication. It's bold, it's fiery, and it absolutely does not apologize.

// TASTING_PROFILE

TASTING NOTES

Aroma

Spicy & Zesty

An immediate hit of raw ginger spice and bright lime zest fills the senses. Behind it, subtle peppery notes from the ginger beer add intrigue.

Palate

Fiery & Tangy

Ginger heat builds from the first sip, immediately tempered by sharp lime acidity. The ginger beer amplifies the spice with effervescent intensity while bitters add an earthy, aromatic backbone.

Finish

Warm & Lingering

A slow, warm ginger burn fades gently with a citrusy aftertaste. The bitters leave a subtle herbal echo that makes you crave the next sip immediately.

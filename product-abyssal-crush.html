Born From

Abyssal Crush was inspired by the idea of diving into something unapologetically bold. We cold-press ripe, sun-ripened watermelon at peak sweetness and marry it with hand-muddled garden mint sourced from local farms. A squeeze of fresh lime cuts through the sweetness, while premium sparkling water adds a lively effervescence that dances on the palate. The result is a drink that feels like a summer sunset in a glass ‚Äî vivid, refreshing, and endlessly sippable. We designed Abyssal Crush for the ones who believe you don't need alcohol to have a drink worth talking about.
